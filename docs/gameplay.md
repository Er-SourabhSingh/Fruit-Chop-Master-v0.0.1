# 🕹️ Gameplay Mechanics

## Fruit Behavior
- Spawn from bottom
- Move upward with random velocity
- Fall due to gravity

## Player Action
- Drag mouse to slice fruits

## Rules
- Slice fruit → +1 score
- Miss fruit → -1 life
- Slice bomb → Game Over

## Difficulty Increase
- Speed increases over time
- More fruits spawn
- Bomb frequency increases
